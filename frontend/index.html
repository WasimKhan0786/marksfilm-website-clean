Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our services

What are your package prices?

+

Our packages range from 20,000 (Normal Shoot) to 1,25,000 (Professional with all gadgets). Each package includes different services:

  • Normal Shoot (20,000): Basic photography & videography
  • Medium Shoot (40,000): Enhanced coverage with editing
  • Premium Shoot (60,000): Professional equipment & creative direction
  • Professional (95,000): Complete wedding coverage
  • Pro + All Gadgets (1,25,000): Premium equipment & unlimited coverage

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes! We offer flexible payment options:

  • 25% advance booking amount
  • 50% before the event
  • 25% after delivery
  • UPI, Cards, Net Banking accepted

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for wedding season (October to March). However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellation policy:

  • 30+ days before: 100% refund
  • 15-30 days: 75% refund
  • 7-15 days: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days: 25% refund

What's included in wedding photography?

+

Our wedding photography includes:

  • Pre-wedding consultation
  • Full day coverage (8-12 hours)
  • Professional editing
  • High-resolution digital gallery
  • Printed album (premium packages)
  • Cinematic highlight video

Do you travel for destination weddings?

+

Absolutely! We love destination weddings. Travel costs are additional and depend on location. We've covered weddings across India and internationally.

How long does editing take?

+

Delivery timeline:

  • Teaser/Highlights: 3-5 days
  • Complete edited photos: 2-3 weeks
  • Full cinematic video: 4-6 weeks
  • Wedding album: 6-8 weeks

What equipment do you use?

+

We use professional-grade equipment:

  • Canon/Sony DSLR & Mirrorless cameras
  • Professional lenses (24-70mm, 70-200mm)
  • 4K video recording capability
  • Drone for aerial shots
  • Professional lighting setup
  • Backup equipment for reliability
